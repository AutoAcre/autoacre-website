How It Works

Set it up once. Enjoy it every day.

Free property assessment

We visit your property, map the terrain, identify mowing zones, garden beds, and any exclusion areas. No obligation, no charge.

Professional deployment

We install the autonomous mowing unit and configure boundary mapping, zone rotation schedules, and safety settings tailored to your property.

Ongoing management

Frequent zone-rotation mowing runs automatically on the mower you own — twice a week in summer, weekly in winter (~78 mow days/year, roughly 3× the cadence of a fortnightly contractor). AutoAcre handles all scheduled maintenance, blade changes, firmware updates and repair coordination as part of the management service.

What's Included

Buy the mower, we manage everything else

Your monthly management fee covers the day-to-day autonomous mowing service — operation, maintenance and repair coordination. Photo reporting and ad-hoc on-site call-outs sit outside the standard fee, so you only pay for what you use.

  • Frequent zone-rotation mowing — ~78 mow days/year (twice-weekly in summer, weekly in winter)

  • Garden bed and exclusion zone configuration during deployment
  • Weekly photo reports available on the higher management tier (quote-only)

Property Requirements

Is your property a good fit?

Property size

4 to 10 acres of mowable area. Larger properties can be assessed for a multi-unit or commercial solution.

Terrain

Handles slopes up to 38°. Most Northern Rivers hinterland properties are well within range, including undulating terrain.

Access & connectivity

The unit needs a sheltered charging station and reasonable cellular coverage for remote monitoring. We assess all of this during the initial site visit.
