Equipment

AutoAcre operates the PANDAG G1, a commercial-grade autonomous robotic mower. Specifications:

  • Daily coverage capacity: up to 25 acres
  • Maximum slope: 38 degrees
  • Navigation: GPS-RTK with cm-level precision
  • Operation: fully autonomous, daily, with remote fleet management
  • Safety: collision detection, lift sensors, geofencing, exclusion-zone mapping
  • Backup unit: zero-turn ride-on supplied by AutoAcre during any repair window

Subscription

The AutoAcre residential autonomous mowing subscription is an all-inclusive monthly service:

  • Price range: $600–$800 per month (AUD), depending on property size and terrain
  • Property size: 0.5 to 3 acres
  • Mowing frequency: daily, every day of the year
  • Includes: autonomous mower, charging dock, all maintenance, blade replacements, firmware updates, weekly photo reports, 7-day repair SLA, zero-turn backup
  • Contract: month-to-month, no long-term lock-in
  • Onboarding: on-site demo before sign-up; demo fee credited to first month

Service area

AutoAcre services the Northern Rivers, NSW — primarily Byron Shire and Ballina Shire:

  • Byron Shire: Byron Bay, Bangalow, Newrybar, Ewingsdale, Mullumbimby, Brunswick Heads, Federal, Myocum, Tyagarah, Brooklet, Clunes, Eureka, Nashua
  • Ballina Shire: Alstonville, Teven, Tintenbar
  • Commercial deployments quoted across the wider Northern Rivers

Commercial capability

AutoAcre is a Northern Rivers managed-service operator using the PANDAG G1 platform. Commercial deployments cover:

  • Local government council vegetation management
  • School and educational grounds
  • Resort and accommodation grounds
  • Golf courses and sports fields
  • Solar farm vegetation management
  • Large rural and lifestyle estates beyond residential subscription scope
