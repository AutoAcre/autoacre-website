Northern Rivers acreage mowing service area

AutoAcre's daily autonomous mowing service covers Byron Shire and Ballina Shire across the Northern Rivers, NSW. Fourteen named suburbs, plus surrounding lifestyle acreage within practical proximity. If your property is anywhere in this region, we can almost certainly help.

Byron Shire

AutoAcre's primary service area. Eleven Byron Shire suburbs covered — predominantly hinterland lifestyle acreage with the rolling terrain that suits autonomous mowing best.

Bangalow

Hinterland village 14 km southwest of Byron Bay. Sought-after lifestyle blocks, rolling terrain.

Ewingsdale

Prestige rural pocket 5 km west of Byron Bay. High-end lifestyle properties, holiday rentals.

Newrybar

Quiet hinterland village 16 km south of Byron. Hilly terrain, established lifestyle acreage.

Mullumbimby

Hinterland town 20 km north of Byron, surrounded by lifestyle and small-farm acreage.

Federal

Deep Byron hinterland 25 km west. Larger holdings, hilly, higher rainfall.

Myocum

Elevated rural pocket 12 km north-northwest of Byron. Long sight-lines, paddock-style properties.

Tyagarah

Coastal hinterland flats 10 km north of Byron. Sandier soils, large open paddocks.

Brooklet

Small hinterland community 18 km southwest of Byron. Well-spaced lifestyle properties.

Clunes

Hinterland village 20 km southwest of Byron. Lifestyle blocks and macadamia country.

Eureka

Quiet hinterland village 22 km west of Byron. Higher-rainfall western Byron Shire.

Nashua

Quiet rural locality 22 km southwest of Byron. Established lifestyle holdings.

Ballina Shire

The southern part of AutoAcre's service area. Three named suburbs covered, plus surrounding lifestyle acreage. Slightly different character — plateau and coastal rather than hinterland — but the service is identical.

Alstonville

Plateau township 12 km west of Ballina. Rich basalt soils drive vigorous grass growth.

Teven

Rural locality 8 km west of Ballina, between Ballina and Alstonville.

Tintenbar

Hilly hinterland 18 km south of Byron, comparable terrain to Newrybar.

Just outside the named suburbs?

The 14 named suburbs are the current core. Properties in adjacent localities — Coorabell, Possum Creek, Goonengerry, Coopers Shoot, Wollongbar, Skennars Head, Knockrow, Bexhill, Suffolk Park, Lennox Head and similar — are typically still within practical service range. The Byron Bay town centre is too built-up for acreage subscriptions but lifestyle-acreage properties on the Byron fringe (Ewingsdale, Tyagarah) absolutely qualify.

Call 0499 649 094 to confirm your specific address. The service area expands periodically as AutoAcre takes on properties in adjacent areas — adding one customer in a new locality typically makes neighbouring properties practical too.

Common service-area questions

What suburbs does AutoAcre service?

14 suburbs across Byron Shire and Ballina Shire: Bangalow, Ewingsdale, Newrybar, Mullumbimby, Federal, Myocum, Tyagarah, Brooklet, Clunes, Eureka, Nashua (Byron Shire), and Alstonville, Teven, Tintenbar (Ballina Shire). Surrounding lifestyle acreage within reasonable proximity is also serviceable on request.

Does AutoAcre service Byron Bay itself?

Byron Bay town centre is mostly residential blocks too small for an acreage subscription. AutoAcre services lifestyle acreage in the Byron hinterland — Ewingsdale, Tyagarah, Bangalow, Newrybar, Federal — surrounding the town. Acreage on the Byron fringe absolutely qualifies.

Does AutoAcre service Ballina town?

Ballina town is mostly residential. AutoAcre services the surrounding Ballina Shire — Alstonville (plateau, 12 km west), Teven (8 km west), Tintenbar (north). Ballina-area lifestyle acreage is within range.

Does AutoAcre service Lismore, Casino or other inland Northern Rivers towns?

Lismore and Casino are outside the current primary area, but properties within reasonable proximity are sometimes serviceable case-by-case. Service area expands periodically — call to check.

Does AutoAcre service Tweed Shire (Tweed Heads, Kingscliff, Murwillumbah)?

Tweed Shire is north of the primary Byron / Ballina area. Some Tweed Shire locations are serviceable depending on proximity — particularly south Tweed and the Murwillumbah corridor — but this is confirmed per-property.

What's the furthest AutoAcre customer from Byron?

The current footprint reaches roughly 25-30 km from Byron Bay in any direction. Beyond that, deployments are case-by-case.

I'm just outside the listed suburbs — can you still service my property?

Often yes. Adjacent localities like Coorabell, Possum Creek, Goonengerry, Coopers Shoot, Wollongbar, Skennars Head are typically within practical range. Call 0499 649 094 to confirm.

Does the service area expand over time?

Yes. The footprint grows as properties in adjacent areas come on board. Adding one customer in a new locality typically makes neighbouring properties practical to service too.
